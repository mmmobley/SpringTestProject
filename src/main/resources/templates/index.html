Welcome to my site dedicated to the best restaurants in San Antonio

This site is all about where you should stop for a bite to eat if you ever visit SA!

Some of the best things about the local food scene in SA are:

  • the variety of cultures has allowed for just as much variety in food
  • there's a saying that "everything is bigger in Texas" and that includes the food
  • Texas is also well known for their barbecue and SA town does not disappoint
  • one word: Tex-Mex!
  • nothing brings more comfort than southern food...BEWARE because it also tends to bring more pounds!
